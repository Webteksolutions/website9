What We Do?

Our Main Aim Is To Educate Underprivileged Children. Join Us for Support!

NDF is one of the top trusted NGO in India, working for women empowerment, Health & child education in rural areas

NDF is a leading charitable organization working with and for older people in India for more than 10 years

Our Vision

  • To ensure that helpless poor children and orphans get educated. Also to help them to find new jobs.
  • To ensure free food to all the poor children and women so that they are healthy and are not malnourished
  • To ensure that any old, poor, or helpless man or woman will have right to stand against any criminal in court and their rights will also be protected.
  • To have an orphanage and old age home together so that orphans can find new parents and the old people can also get some help.

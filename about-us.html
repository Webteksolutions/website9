New Dream Foundation NGO

New Dream foundation is an NGO whose journey started back in 2016 when our chairman helped a lot of poor and helpless people by providing them all sorts of legal and educational help. He also provided healthcare facilities like free medicines. After seeing his dedication towards the societal work which he was doing tirelessly, a lot of people joined hands with our chairman and then in 2018, New Dream Foundation was legally registered.

Our organisation always wanted to help the needy and old aged people. We wanted to educate the helpless children and also wanted to provide legal and health care facilities to poor people. We also wanted to open an orphanage and an old age home nearby. But all of this required a lot of money, so we thought that why not generate money by ourselves with the help of activities like farming, cowshed, etc.

After we generated some amount of funds, we started giving free medicines to all the helpless and poor people, especially women and children. Then we started giving free legal support to the poor and elderly people as well so that their rights can be protected

Our Main Goals Are-

  • Providing food to poor women and children
  • Providing jobs to women and youth
  • Finding out solutions to stop domestic violence
  • Finding out solutions to stop the abortions of girl child
  • Educating orphans and poor children

Our Mission

  • Our mission is to meet all our aims and also to do our job i.e., to help the poor, needy and helpless old people with utmost potential.
  • We also provide them with free legal help, education, food, shelter, medical healthcare, etc.
  • We try to reach out as many people as we can so that we can spread awareness so that everybody knows us and anybody can approach us for help without facing any difficulties.
